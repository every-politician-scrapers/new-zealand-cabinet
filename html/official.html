Notes:

  • all Ministers are members of the Executive Council
  • the Parliamentary Under-Secretaries are part of executive government, but are not members of the Executive Council
  • portfolios are listed in the left-hand column. Other responsibilities assigned by the Prime Minister are listed in the right-hand column.
